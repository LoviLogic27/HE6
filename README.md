SAFE SIGNAL is a mobile app that helps people stay safe by sending instant SOS alerts and sharing live location with trusted contacts.

IF YOUR PHONE BLOCKS THE SMS FUNCTION OR THE LOCATION BECAUSE IS NOT VERIFIED BY GOOGLE HERE'S WHAT TO DO:

On your Android device, open the Settings app.
Tap Apps.
Tap the app that you want to turn on a restricted setting for.
Tip: If you can't find it, first tap See all apps or App info.
Tap More  and then Allow restricted settings.
Follow the on-screen instructions.
